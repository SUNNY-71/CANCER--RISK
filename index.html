About Project

Cancer Risk & Pollution Tracker is a web-based platform designed to create awareness about cancer risk factors such as air pollution, contaminated water and unhealthy lifestyle habits. The platform helps users monitor pollution levels, track health activities and receive suggestions to reduce health risks.

🫁 Lung Cancer

Lung Cancer Cause: Smoking, air pollution Symptoms: Continuous cough, chest pain Prevention: Avoid smoking, stay in clean air

☀ Skin Cancer

Skin Cancer Cause: UV radiation, harmful chemicals Symptoms: Skin spots, unusual growth Prevention: Use sunscreen, avoid direct sunlight

🎗 Breast Cancer Cause: Hormonal changes, lifestyle Symptoms: Lump in breast, pain Prevention: Regular health checkup
